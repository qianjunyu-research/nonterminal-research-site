Architecture Surface

SOE is organized through three current structure surfaces: Meta-Governance, Governance Architecture, and Operation Layer. Together, they frame governance as a revisable architecture rather than a final state, incorporating simulation findings and future-concern pattern research into the structure itself.

Problem-Domain Framing

SOE can also be entered through concrete concern domains: AI and work, survival cost, wealth concentration, institutional decay, shared-reality collapse, surveillance, war escalation, social fragmentation, moral disagreement, and long-term stability. These pages do not claim that SOE solves those domains. They provide a communication route from recognizable public concerns to the architecture documents and simulation-bounded evidence classes.
