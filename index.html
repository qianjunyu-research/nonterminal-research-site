About

QianJun Yu develops independent theoretical work on systems that must remain coherent without a terminal authority. The research portfolio spans the System of Eternity, speculative cosmology, cognitive architecture, and related formal systems.

The public archive currently lives on Zenodo. This site prototype is a front door for readers who need a clear map of the work before entering individual papers and datasets.

Research Programs

System of Eternity

A civilizational governance architecture for continuity, adjudication, evidence boundaries, and non-terminal institutional revision.

CGEM

Speculative cosmology work exploring constrained models of large-scale density gradients, field interactions, and possible observational deviations.

Trinity Framework

A cognitive architecture model using instinct, reason, and oversight constraints to examine human-scale governance interfaces.
