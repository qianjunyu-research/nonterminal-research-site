# Non-Terminal Research Social Assets

These images use the accepted Non-Terminal Research visual identity: ink-navy field, warm paper typography, bronze rule, and a restrained `NT` wordmark.

## Files

- `nonterminal-og.png` - 1200 x 630 Open Graph / large social preview image.
- `nonterminal-linkedin-banner.png` - 1584 x 396 LinkedIn profile background candidate.
- `nonterminal-wide-banner.png` - 1500 x 500 general banner candidate for profile headers or future platform use.
- `source/nonterminal-social-assets.html` - reproducible HTML source used to render the PNG assets with headless Chrome.

## Boundary

These assets are public identity assets only. They do not alter the research status, evidence boundaries, publication claims, or deployment/readiness status of any SOE or CTF materials.
