# SOE Research Hub Website Prototype Notes

Date: 2026-06-02

## Purpose

This prototype turns the current visual direction into a concrete static website surface for review:

- institutional research hub
- selected publication entry point
- SOE overview page
- public writing bridge
- status and evidence-boundary page
- contact placeholder pending domain decision

## Visual Direction

The site follows the current multi-AI research consensus:

- dark ink-navy opening band
- warm off-white paper body
- restrained bronze and steel-blue accents
- serif headings with clean sans-serif body text
- abstract layer/ring/node motif
- quiet structural dividers
- homepage identity framed as Non-Terminal Research, with QianJun Yu retained as author/researcher
- no startup gradient, no sci-fi imagery, no political styling, no cult-like symbol

## Boundary
